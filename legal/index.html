1. Legal Status​

Ethical & Secure Practices Alliance (E.S.P.A.) is a Swiss Association (Verein) established under Articles 60–79 of the Swiss Civil Code.

The registered office of the association is located in the Canton of Zug, Switzerland. The association may relocate its registered office within Switzerland in accordance with its statutes.

2. Purpose​

E.S.P.A. is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting ethical, secure, and privacy-respecting practices in digital systems and technologies.

The association focuses on education, awareness, open source development, and community collaboration related to responsible technology use.

3. Activities & Assets​

To fulfill its purpose, the association may develop, maintain, and steward open source software, documentation, and educational materials.

E.S.P.A. may own, manage, license, and enforce intellectual property, including software, documentation, trademarks, and other digital assets, including those hosted on third-party platforms such as GitHub.
