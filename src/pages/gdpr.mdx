## GDPR Information

Ethical & Secure Practices Alliance (E.S.P.A.) is committed to respecting data protection principles under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## Lawful Basis

Where personal data is processed, it is based on:
- Legitimate interest
- Voluntary communication initiated by the user

---

## Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR, individuals may have the right to:
- Access their data
- Request correction
- Request deletion
- Object to processing

Rights depend on the nature of the data processed.

---

## Data Minimization

E.S.P.A. follows a principle of collecting **only what is necessary** and avoiding unnecessary personal data processing.

---

## International Access

ESPA Docs is accessible globally. Data processing practices aim to respect privacy protections regardless of location.
