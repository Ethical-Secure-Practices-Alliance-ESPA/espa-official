## GDPR Information

Ethical & Secure Practices Alliance (E.S.P.A.) is a Swiss association committed to respecting data protection principles under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Swiss data protection law (nFADP).

---

## Data Controller

The data controller responsible for processing personal data is:

**Ethical & Secure Practices Alliance (E.S.P.A.)**  
Switzerland  
Contact: **info@myespa.ch**

---

## Lawful Basis for Processing

Where personal data is processed, the lawful basis may include:

- Legitimate interest (e.g., site security, abuse prevention)  
- Voluntary communication initiated by the user (e.g., email inquiries)

No data is collected or processed for commercial profiling or marketing.

---

## Types of Data Processed

Minimal data may be processed, such as:

- Technical/log data (IP addresses anonymized where possible)  
- Email messages sent voluntarily to ESPA

---

## Data Subject Rights

Individuals may have the right to:

- Access personal data held by ESPA  
- Request correction or deletion  
- Object to processing where applicable  

These rights depend on the nature of the data processed and applicable law.

---

## Data Minimization

ESPA follows the principle of collecting **only what is necessary** and avoiding unnecessary personal data processing.

---

## International Access

ESPA Docs is publicly accessible worldwide. Data handling practices aim to respect privacy protections regardless of location.

---

## Contact for GDPR Requests

All GDPR-related inquiries may be directed to:

**info@myespa.ch**

If concerns cannot be resolved directly, individuals may contact the relevant data protection authority in Switzerland or their jurisdiction.

---
