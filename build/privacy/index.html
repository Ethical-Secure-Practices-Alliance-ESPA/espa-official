Privacy Policy​

Ethical & Secure Practices Alliance (E.S.P.A.) respects privacy by design.

This Privacy Policy explains how ESPA Docs handles information.

Data Collection​

ESPA Docs:

  • Does not require user accounts for public access
  • Does not sell personal data
  • Does not engage in behavioral advertising

Minimal technical data may be processed for:

  • Site security
  • Performance
  • Abuse prevention

Cookies and Analytics​

ESPA Docs aims to minimize tracking.

If analytics are used, they are:

  • Privacy-respecting
  • Aggregated
  • Non-identifying

Where applicable, consent mechanisms are respected.

Third-Party Services​

Some services (such as hosting or content delivery) may process limited technical data.

E.S.P.A. does not control third-party privacy practices.

Email Communication​

If you contact ESPA via email:

  • Your message is used only to respond
  • No marketing lists are created
  • No data is shared externally

Data Retention​

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose or comply with applicable obligations.

Your Rights​

You may request:

  • Clarification on data handling
  • Correction or deletion of personal data (where applicable)

Requests may be sent to:

info@myespa.ch
