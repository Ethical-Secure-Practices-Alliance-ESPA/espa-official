Who We Serve

ESPA is dedicated to supporting two main groups of users: home users and NGOs/Organizations. Our mission is to provide practical, actionable, and ethical guidance to help each group navigate digital security, privacy, and ethical technology practices.

Home Users​

Home users include individuals, families, or anyone managing technology for personal use. Our documentation provides clear, step-by-step guidance on topics such as network security, account management, privacy, and secure storage.

Home users can expect:

  • Easy-to-follow explanations that make complex concepts approachable.
  • Actionable steps to improve personal digital safety immediately.
  • Guidance that empowers them to make informed decisions about the tools and services they use.

By following ESPA’s public guides, home users become part of a larger community committed to ethical and secure practices. Later, if they choose, they can support ESPA by accessing supporter-funded advanced guides, helping us continue to produce free, high-quality resources.

NGOs / Organizations​

NGOs, non-profits, and other organizations face unique challenges in digital security and data protection. ESPA’s documentation helps these groups:

  • Implement organizational privacy and security practices.
  • Manage sensitive data responsibly.
  • Train team members on ethical and secure technology practices.

Our guides provide general guidance designed to be applicable across a variety of organizations. Employees and members should always follow their organization’s specific policies, while using ESPA’s documentation as a framework for improving security and privacy practices.

By engaging with ESPA’s resources, NGOs and organizations contribute to a culture of ethical and secure technology, and those who later choose to support ESPA through advanced guides help ensure that these resources remain free, updated, and widely available.
