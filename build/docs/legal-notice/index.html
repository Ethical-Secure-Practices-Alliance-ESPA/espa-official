1. Legal Status​

Ethical & Secure Practices Alliance (E.S.P.A.) is a Swiss association (Verein) established under Articles 60–79 of the Swiss Civil Code (ZGB).

Registered office: Canton of Zug, Switzerland
The association may relocate its registered office within Switzerland in accordance with its statutes.

2. Purpose​

E.S.P.A. is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting ethical, secure, and privacy-respecting practices in digital systems and technologies.

The association focuses on:

  • Education and awareness
  • Open source development
  • Community collaboration related to responsible technology use
