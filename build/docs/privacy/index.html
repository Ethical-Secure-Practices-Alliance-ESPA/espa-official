Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy​

Ethical & Secure Practices Alliance (E.S.P.A.) is a Swiss association committed to privacy by design and data minimization.

This Privacy Policy explains how ESPA Docs handles information when you access or interact with this website.

Data Controller​

The data controller responsible for this website is:

Ethical & Secure Practices Alliance (E.S.P.A.)
Switzerland
Contact: info@myespa.ch

Data Collection​

ESPA Docs is designed to be accessed without the creation of user accounts.

ESPA Docs:

  • Does not require user registration for public access
  • Does not sell or commercialize personal data
  • Does not engage in behavioral advertising or profiling

Limited technical data may be processed for:

  • Site security and abuse prevention
  • Performance and reliability
  • Operational integrity

Where possible, such data is anonymized or aggregated.

Cookies and Analytics​

ESPA Docs aims to minimize tracking technologies.

If analytics are used, they are:

  • Privacy-respecting
  • Aggregated
  • Non-identifying

Where required by law, consent mechanisms are respected.

Third-Party Services​

ESPA Docs may rely on third-party services (such as hosting, content delivery, or security providers) that process limited technical data.

ESPA does not control the privacy practices of third parties. Processing by such providers is subject to their respective privacy policies and applicable legal safeguards.

ESPA may use content delivery and security services to protect against abuse and ensure availability.

Email Communication​

If you contact ESPA via email:

  • Your message is used solely to respond to your inquiry
  • No marketing or mailing lists are created
  • Information is not shared externally unless required by law

Data Retention​

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill its intended purpose or to comply with applicable legal obligations.

Your Rights​

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request information about how personal data is handled
  • Request correction or deletion of personal data, where applicable

Requests may be submitted to:

info@myespa.ch

If concerns cannot be resolved directly, you may have the right to contact the relevant data protection authority.

Changes to This Policy​

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes.
The current version will always be published on this website.
